Chain

Expiremental library for safe method chaining. 

  person = "John Doe"
  def person.address = "123 West St."
  person.address.street #=> Error

To avoid the error use chain: 

  person.chain.address.street.end   #=> nil

Or 

  ~ person.chain.address.street     #=> nil
