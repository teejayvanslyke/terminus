Random

Randomization core extension methods.

This library extends Object, Array, String and Hash with randomization methods. Most of the methods are of one of two kinds. Either they "pick" a random element from the reciever or they randomly "shuffle" the reciever.

The most common example is Array#shuffle, which simply randmomizes the order of an array‘s elements. 

  [1,2,3].shuffle  #=> [2,3,1]

The other methods do similar things for their respective classes.
